About Ethan

I am a pianist from Dublin, Ireland. I am heavily influenced by Ludovico Einaudi and Sigur Ros creating music somewhere in between.

I love music. I love creating it, listening to it, playing it.
To me music is so much more than the sounds being produced. It is an emotional journey and speaks directly to the soul.
It can make you happy, it can make you sad. It can relax you, it can excite you. It can move you in ways you never thought possible.
It is an orgasmic experience for me. And I am addicted to it.

I try to make music that connects to you on an emotional level. Using a more minimalist feel and focusing on melodies that bring you on an emotional journey and moves your soul.
And fundamentally I hope that my music can bring you a moment of joy.
