About Ethan David

Not quite sure where I'm from, but wherever I go, I do love to make a lot of noise!

I'm a entrepreneur/physicist/treasure hunter based in Dublin, Ireland. But I spend most of my days (and nights) disturbing my neighbours constantly playing music way too loud.

What started out with pots and pans and toy guitars, turned into something slightly more pleasing to the ear than random noise. All those years of piano lessons starting to pay off!

In my spare time I like to breath fire, practice my jedi skills and play with lego.
*N.B. - One of these claims is a lie.
